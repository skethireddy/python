Bug ID 420290: AVR issues with timezone and time adjustments

Published: 2013-05-01 02:06:11  Last Modified: 2018-02-26 10:17:14

Bug Tracker

Product and Affected Module:
BIG-IP AVR

Known Affected Versions:
11.4.0, 11.3.0

Fixed In:
11.5.0

Opened: 2013-05-01 02:06:11
Severity: 3-Major
Related Known Issue Article: /support.f5.com/csp/#/article/K10473262
 Vulnerability Severity:
CVE:
Related Security Advisory Article: /support.f5.com/csp/#/article/K10473262

Symptoms

Impact: When the timezone (System > Platform > Time Zone) is in any time zone under WEST (for example, the time zone for "Europe/Lisbon"), or EST, the time shown in the Reporting > DoS > Application for the "Time Period" of "Last Hour" is an hour ahead of the time system.

Impact

AVR reports and graph data dose not make sense

Conditions

Time change to past on already working system.

Workaround

This issue has no workaround at this time.

Fix Information

Fix logic conditions regarding time chance.
